Curry

Description

A dish composed with a sauce or gravy seasoned with a mixture of ground spices that is thought to have originated in India and has since spread to many regions of the world.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Vegetable
  • Curry powder
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Coconut milk
  • Garlic
  • Chili pepper
  • Salt
  • Ginger

Steps:

  1. Brown the meta
  2. Sweat the onions
  3. Bloom the spices
  4. Simmer the curry
  5. Enjoy!
