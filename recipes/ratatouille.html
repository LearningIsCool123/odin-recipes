Ratatouille

Description

A seasoned stew made of eggplant, tomatoes, green peppers, squash, and sometimes meat.

Ingredients

  • Tomato
  • Aubergine
  • Courgette
  • Bell pepper
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Roast nuts
  2. Prepare eggplants
  3. Slice onions and cook them
  4. Add tomato puree
  5. Cook peppers
